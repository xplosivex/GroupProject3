How would you gain consensus and what level of involvement should the board of directors have?

Consensus will be gained when the CEO, Board of Directors, management, senior staff, and CISO all agree. Once everyone is on the same page and in universal agreement, consensus has been achieved. The Board of Directors themselves must approve the classification of information and the authorization of protection levels.
